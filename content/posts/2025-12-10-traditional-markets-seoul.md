## FAQ

![FAQ](/images/traditional-markets-seoul-faq-0.webp)


**Q: What are traditional markets in Seoul?**

They are bustling markets where locals shop for food, clothes, and street snacks, showcasing everyday Seoul life.

**Q: Which markets are must-visit?**

Key markets include Gwangjang, Namdaemun, Dongdaemun, Noryangjin, Tongin, Majang, and Mangwon.

**Q: What should I bring to the markets?**

Bring small bills and coins, comfortable shoes, and a polite attitude for bargaining and sampling.

**Q: Is it customary to tip at markets?**

No, tipping is not customary in Korea; the price displayed is the price you pay.

**Q: Can I take photos in the markets?**

Yes, but it's courteous to ask vendors before taking close-up shots.
